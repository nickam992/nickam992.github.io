Getting Started

Grouping

Grouping patterns make them easier to maintain and transport. A group usually contains a group-title and at least one editor pattern. Even this section is a group.

<div class="group">
	<h2 class="group-title">Group Title</h2>
	<input type="text" placeholder="Placeholder Text">
</div>

Groups can also be useful for associating buttons with a particular module. The buttons to the right are nested within the group, making it easier to move them around together.

Editor Workspace

The main body of the editor consists of a single column workspace. Patterns are added to this workspace to build an editor interface.

The code below is the minimum HTML to setup the workspace.

<body class="editor">
	<div class="workspace">
		<div class="content">
			<!-- Add your content here -->
		</div>
	</div>
</body>

Control Panel

Some widgets call for an aditional side panel to help users navigate through aditional content or change settings.

<body class="editor panel--active">
	<div class="panel">
		<!-- Add your panel content here -->
	</div>
	<div class="workspace">
		<div class="content">
			<!-- Add your content here -->
		</div>
	</div>
</body>
